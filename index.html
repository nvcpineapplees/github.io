Practicing NVC for free

Every Saturday morning (9am - 11am CET online) we come together to practice certain NVC exercises. Our aim is to have this NVC community live on in a sustainable way and to have it remain a valuable part of our lives.

The art of empathy

Each morning Monday to Friday (8am - 8:30am CET online) we provide the opportunity to start the day with a duet empathy session.

In real life meetups

Once a month we will meetup in Amsterdam. If you'd like this practice group to initiate IRL meetups closer to where you are, please let us know :)

The potential

When we have a community of people inspired by NVC that stay connected through this practice group, we can initiate projects together. From NVC retreats, to an NVC youtube channel, practices and maybe even some crazy ambitous dreams like a commune or school.
