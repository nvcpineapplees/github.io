The essence

The famous 4 steps in NVC

1

Observation

What is reality? What happened in terms in which no discussion is possible?

2

Feelings

What emotions and feelings are present? Anger? Sadness? Gratitude? Restlessness?

3

Needs

What is it that you as a human so much want right now? What is it that you need? Connection? Safety? Trust? Clarity?

4

Request

Is there a clear doable action that you would like someone to do? Something you can request that you think would contribute to these needs of yours being fulfilled.
